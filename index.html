Comprehensive Weekly Cleaning Checklist for Co-working Space

Monday & Wednesday (Basic) | Tuesday & Thursday (Full) | Friday (Deep)

Offices

Task Mon (Basic) Tue (Full) Wed (Basic) Thu (Full) Fri (Deep)
Empty bins & replace liners
Vacuum & spot clean floors/carpets
Dust desks & chairs
Sanitize keyboards & shared electronics (Full/Deep)
Clean windowsills & remove cobwebs (Deep only)
Clean behind desks & chairs (Deep only)
Wipe light switches & door handles (Basic every day)

Kitchen

Task Mon (Basic) Tue (Full) Wed (Basic) Thu (Full) Fri (Deep)
Wipe counters & tables
Clean sink & faucets
Empty bins & replace liners
Wipe exterior of fridge & microwave
Clean inside fridge (Full & Deep)
Clean inside microwave thoroughly (Deep only)

Bathrooms

Task Mon (Basic) Tue (Full) Wed (Basic) Thu (Full) Fri (Deep)
Clean & disinfect toilets
Restock toilet paper, soap, towels
Clean sinks, mirrors, faucets
Disinfect stalls & partitions (Full & Deep)
Scrub grout & floor drains (Deep only)
