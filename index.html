Detailed Weekly Cleaning Checklist

Each day has its own cleaning focus: Monday & Wednesday (Normal), Tuesday & Thursday (Full), Friday (Deep Clean).

Common Areas

Task Mon (Normal) Tue (Full) Wed (Normal) Thu (Full) Fri (Deep)

Dust & wipe surfaces (tables, seats)
Sanitize high-touch points (door handles, switches)

Meeting Rooms

Task Mon (Normal) Tue (Full) Wed (Normal) Thu (Full) Fri (Deep)
Wipe tables & chairs
Clean screens & shared electronics

Kitchens

Task Mon (Normal) Tue (Full) Wed (Normal) Thu (Full) Fri (Deep)
Clean appliances (microwave, fridge)
Wipe counters, tables, chairs
Empty bins, replace liners

Bathrooms

Task Mon (Normal) Tue (Full) Wed (Normal) Thu (Full) Fri (Deep)
Clean & disinfect toilets
Restock supplies (toilet paper, soap)
Clean mirrors, sinks & faucets
